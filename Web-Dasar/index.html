Gibson Les Paul

The Gibson Les Paul is a legendary electric guitar known for its rich, warm tone and outstanding sustain, featuring a solid mahogany body with a carved maple top, dual humbucking pickups, and a set neck design that enhances resonance and playability.

The Fender Stratocaster, often referred to as the "Strat," is an iconic electric guitar renowned for its distinctive double-cutaway body shape, three single-coil pickups, and a versatile five-way selector switch that allows for a wide range of tonal possibilities.
